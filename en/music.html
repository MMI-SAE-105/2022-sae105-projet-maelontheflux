Music that does justice to his work...

Shingeki No Kyojin,
Attack of the Titans.

The name of the anime is Shingeki No Kiojin.
We are talking about Guren no Yumiya, a musical representation of the story of Attack of the Titans.

This anime was released in 2013, it is composed of 4 seasons. It tells the story of a world affected by a difficult war for many centuries.

This great battle against the cannibal giants and the humans who survived these long centuries. They survive in their village called "Paradise", hidden behind three great walls of protection.

First, the wall closest to the outside.
The Maria wall, then the Rose wall and the Sina wall are the closest to the humans.

Guren no yumiya means "The scarlet bow and arrow". He uses the term scarlet to make it less violent

The impact of the expression is thus slightly reduced, so it means "The bloody bow and arrow".
The instrumental of the music is based on low notes and fairly fast notes.

The music makes one feel violence.

The lyrics are about the feeling of revenge and the feeling that people have when they see their lives unjustly destroyed.

The story of Guren no yumiya.

Guren no Yumiya performed by Linked Horizon.

A Japanese music group created by the author and Revo in 2012.

It consists of 13 members including 7 singers, 5 musicians and 2 composers.

It is a Japanese pop & rock band.

It has composed three of the five opening music of the Japanese animation series "Shingeki No Kyojin" in French "L'Attaque des Titans".

Guren no Yumiya was released on July 10, 2013 in the album Jiyū e no Shingeki dedicated to Attack of the Titans.

It is an album with music of the symphonic metal genre.
The lyrics are sung in Japanese and German.

On Spotify, the music, which lasts 5 minutes and 10 seconds, already has more than 120 million listens.
The Youtube video of the music Guren no yumiya with the opening credits has 40 million views.
