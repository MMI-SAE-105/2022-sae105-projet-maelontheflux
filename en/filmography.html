Mel Gibson Filmography

Some films...

Who is Mel Gibson?

Mel Gibson, was born on 3 January 1956 in Peekskill, USA.
He is an American actor, director, screenwriter and film producer.

He spent part of his childhood in Australia. He became known for the role of Max in Mad Max.

He is one of the most profitable actors in film history.

Hacksaw Ridge

Thou shalt not kill, is a dramatic film released in November 2016.

It tells the story of a soldier whose goal is to heal his battalion by not carrying a gun to the front, the story is based on a true story.

Mel Gibson is the director and the lead actor is Andrew Garfield.

BRAVEHEART

BRAVEHEART, released in 1995, is a dramatic film.

It tells the story of William Wallace, a hero who fought for Scottish independence in the 13th century by fighting the English forces.

Mel Gibson is both the actor and the director.

Lethal Weapon

Lethal Weapon was released in 1987 and is an action and crime film.
It is part of the Deadly Alarm saga.

The film tells the story of two partners in the American police force.

Roger Murtaugh has to deal with his suicidal partner Martin Riggs, their only connection being the Vietnam War. So Roger Murtaugh will try to solve his partner's problems.

The director is Richard Donner and Mel Gibson stars Martin Riggs.

Lethal Weapon 2

Lethal Weapon 2, released in 1987, is an action and crime film.
It is part of the Lethal Alert saga.

Martin Riggs and Roger Murtaugh are at it again.
Their goal is to protect a former bandit who has betrayed drug dealers by testifying.

In doing so, they follow a trail that leads them to the South African consulate.

Mel Gibson is the main actor and the director is Richard Donner.

Lethal Weapon 3

Lethal Weapon 3, released in 1992, is an action, comedy and crime film.
It is part of the Lethal Weapon saga.

The return of Roger Murtaugh and his partner Sergeant Martin Riggs is set up by Jack Travis. Jack Travis is a former policeman and the leader of a gang that sells guns.
Sergeants Roger Murtaugh and Martin Riggs must fight a man who knows his stuff.

Richard Donner is the director and Mel Gibson stars.

Apocalypto

Apocalypto was released in 2006 and is an action and adventure film.

Jaguar Paw is the son of the chief of his tribe. One day Mayan warriors attack his village to capture human sacrifices.
When he is captured by the enemy tribe, he does everything he can to save his land.

Mel gibson is the director and Rudy Youngblood is the lead actor.

Mad Max

Mad Max, was released in 1979 and is a post-apocalyptic science fiction film.
It is part of the Mad Max saga.

A criminal escapes by killing a policeman and stealing a police car. Several policemen try to arrest him, but Max Rockatansky takes up the chase.

The director is George Miller and Mel Gibson stars as Max.

Mad Max 2: The Challenge

MAD Max 2: The Challenge was released in 1981 and is a post-apocalyptic science fiction film.
It is part of the Mad Max saga.

In the future, oil has run out and the new law is violence.

A former employee of the Transportation Security Administration named Max is accompanied by an Australian cattle dog. He helps bandits who are fighting street pirates. They fight near a refinery tank.

The director is George Miller and Mel Gibson stars as Max.

We were soldiers

We Were Soldiers, was released in 2002 and is an action film about the Vietnam War.

Lieutenant Colonel Hal Moore is given officers to train and educate. When they are in Vietnam, Moore finds that they are missing information.
They parachute in anyway and fall into the "Valley of Death", a point controlled by the enemy. They are quickly surrounded by the enemy.

The director is Randall Wallace and Mel Gibson stars Hal Moore.
